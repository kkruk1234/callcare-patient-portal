TITLE: Medication Renewal Protocol (Prototype)

INCLUSION:
- Patient requests renewal/refill of an existing medication previously prescribed.
- Patient confirms currently taking the medication as directed.

EXCLUSIONS (route to clinician review):
- New or worsening symptoms
- Concerning side effects or adverse reaction
- Any emergency red flags (chest pain, severe dyspnea, stroke symptoms, severe bleeding, syncope, self-harm)
- Pregnancy risk (if relevant)
- Controlled substances excluded in v1
- Unclear medication name/dose or unclear adherence

DISPOSITION:
- If inclusion met and no exclusions: eligible for Tier-1 protocol auto-finalization.
- Otherwise: Tier-2 physician review required.
